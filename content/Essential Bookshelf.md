## Must-Read (in order)

| # | Book | Focus | Level |
|---|------|-------|------|
| 1 | [[Trading and Exchanges — Larry Harris]] | Market mechanics from zero | 1 |
| 2 | [[Introduction to Probability — Blitzstein & Hwang]] | Probability from first principles (free Harvard PDF) | 1 |
| 3 | [[Introduction to Probability Models — Sheldon Ross]] | Probability and stochastic processes | 1-2 |
| 4 | [[Stochastic Calculus for Finance II — Steven Shreve]] | The mathematical backbone | 2 |
| 5 | [[Algorithmic Trading — Ernie Chan]] | Practical stat arb with code | 3 |
| 6 | [[Algorithmic and High-Frequency Trading — Cartea et al.]] | Extends [[Avellaneda-Stoikov]] | 3-6 |

## High Priority

| 8 | [[Introduction to Linear Algebra — Gilbert Strang]] | Eigenvalues, PCA, quadratic forms (pair with MIT 18.06 lectures) | 1-2 |
| 9 | [[Convex Optimization — Boyd & Vandenberghe]] | Optimization theory, chapters 1-5 (free Stanford PDF) | 2 |

| 11 | [[Advances in Financial Machine Learning — López de Prado]] | Making ML work in finance | 6 |
| 12 | [[Quantitative Risk Management — McNeil, Frey & Embrechts]] | Proper risk modeling | 3, 6 |
| 13 | [[Trades, Quotes and Prices — Bouchaud et al.]] | Empirical market microstructure | 6 |
| 14 | [[Introduction to Statistical Learning (ISLR)]] | ML fundamentals (free PDF) | 3, 6 |

- Crack, "Heard on the Street" — classic quant interview prep
- Joshi, "Quant Job Interview Questions" — broader question set
- [QuantGuide.io](https://quantguide.io) — LeetCode for quants
- [Brainstellar](https://brainstellar.com) — probability and logic puzzles
