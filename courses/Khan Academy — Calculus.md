## Key Takeaways

- Partial derivatives are essential — options depend on price, time, and volatility simultaneously, each partial derivative is a Greek
- The chain rule extends to multiple variables and is the foundation of Itô's lemma (with a stochastic correction)
- Integration techniques matter for computing expected values, pricing integrals, and cumulative distribution functions
- Taylor series to second order is enough for most finance — delta-gamma approximation is just a Taylor expansion
