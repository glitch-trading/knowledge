# Khan Academy — Statistics & Probability

**Free. Alternative to [[Harvard Stat 110]] for a lighter introduction.**

## What It Covers

Random variables, probability distributions, expected value, variance, standard deviation, conditional probability, independence, Bayes' theorem. Good for building foundations before the more rigorous [[Introduction to Probability — Blitzstein & Hwang]].

## Links

- [Statistics & Probability](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability)

## Key Takeaways

- Expected value is the probability-weighted average outcome — the single number that summarizes a gamble
- Variance measures dispersion around the mean — in finance, variance is risk
- Conditional probability changes everything — markets are about updating beliefs given new information
- Independence is a strong assumption that rarely holds in finance — correlations spike in crises
